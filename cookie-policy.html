Cookie Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026 · Aisle Sage, Ontario, Canada · Questions: info@aislesage.com

We keep our cookie use minimal and purposeful. We use cookies only to make the service work properly — not to track you across the web or serve you ads.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide information to site owners. Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or install malware.

2. How We Use Cookies

Aisle Sage uses cookies strictly to operate the service. We do not use cookies for advertising, cross-site tracking, or selling data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use to operate Aisle Sage:

Supabase

Supabase sets authentication cookies to manage your login session securely. These are essential for the service to function.

Stripe

When you proceed to checkout, Stripe may set cookies on their payment pages to prevent fraud and process your payment securely. These cookies are governed by Stripe's Cookie Policy.

5. What We Do NOT Use Cookies For

  • Advertising or retargeting
  • Tracking you across other websites
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Building advertising profiles
  • Analytics beyond what is necessary to operate the service

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Here is how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to Aisle Sage and using core features of the service.

7. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, Aisle Sage uses your browser's local storage to save your wedding plan data locally. This allows your plan to be restored quickly when you return. Local storage data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except when you are logged in.

You can clear local storage through your browser's developer tools or by clearing your browser data.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of Aisle Sage after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
