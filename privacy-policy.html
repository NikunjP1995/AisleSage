Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026 · Aisle Sage, Ontario, Canada · Questions: info@aislesage.com

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We don't sell your data. We never will.

1. Who We Are

Aisle Sage is a sole proprietorship registered in Ontario, Canada, operating the AI-powered wedding planning service at aislesage.com. When this policy refers to "Aisle Sage", "we", "us", or "our", it means the Aisle Sage business and its services.

You can reach us at any time at info@aislesage.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Account information — your name, partner's name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Wedding details — wedding date, location, budget, guest count, and style preferences you share with our AI planner
  • Guest list information — names and email addresses of wedding guests you add to your planning dashboard
  • Conversation history — messages you send to Sage, our AI wedding planner, to enable continuity across sessions
  • Feedback — ratings and comments you submit through our feedback widget

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • IP address — used to determine approximate location for venue recommendations
  • Cookies — session cookies to keep you logged in and preference cookies to remember your settings

Information we do NOT collect

  • Payment card numbers, CVV codes, or bank account details — all payments are processed by Stripe, which has its own privacy policy
  • Government identification or financial records
  • Sensitive personal information beyond what is needed for wedding planning

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Aisle Sage service
  • Power our AI wedding planner — your wedding details are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalized recommendations
  • Restore your wedding plan when you log back in
  • Send account-related emails such as password resets and billing notifications
  • Respond to your support requests and feedback
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

Aisle Sage uses the following third-party services to operate:

Anthropic (AI Processing)

When you chat with Sage, your messages and wedding details are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Anthropic's privacy practices at anthropic.com.

Supabase (Database and Authentication)

Your account information and wedding plan data are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database provider. Supabase stores data in servers located in the United States.

Stripe (Payments)

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Aisle Sage never receives, stores, or has access to your payment card information. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

Vercel (Hosting)

Our website is hosted on Vercel, which processes web traffic data including IP addresses and request logs.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • Wedding plan data is retained for 2 years after your last active session
  • Conversation history with Sage is retained for 12 months
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by Canadian tax law

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@aislesage.com.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — request your wedding plan data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@aislesage.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Aisle Sage is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at info@aislesage.com.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Row-level security policies on our database
  • Secure, hashed password storage via Supabase Auth
  • API keys stored as server-side environment variables, never exposed in client code

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies

We use cookies to operate the service. Specifically:

  • Session cookies — keep you logged in during your visit
  • Authentication cookies — set by Supabase to maintain your login state across sessions
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings such as plan preferences

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect your ability to use Aisle Sage.

10. International Data Transfers

Aisle Sage is based in Ontario, Canada. Your data may be processed and stored in the United States through our service providers (Supabase, Vercel, Anthropic, Stripe). By using Aisle Sage, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a prominent notice on our website at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of Aisle Sage after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
